# Main Image Rubric

## Slot 01 MAIN Hard Rejects

Reject from slot `01` when the image has:

- Non-white or visibly tinted/scene background unless category rules allow it.
- Text overlays, icons, badges, measurements, logos, watermarks, or promotional claims.
- Props/accessories not included in the offer, unless unambiguously contextual and category-allowed.
- Lifestyle scene, model/hand use, packaging-only photo, collage, multi-panel layout, or comparison graphic.
- Wrong variant, wrong quantity, mismatched color, cropped product, heavy blur, low resolution, or unclear product identity.
- Image that depends on invented claims or claims not supported by listing evidence.

## Slot 01 Score

Use 0-100:

- 30 identity and variant match.
- 20 compliance cleanliness.
- 20 clarity, resolution, focus, and full-product framing.
- 15 thumbnail click strength.
- 10 completeness of included parts/set.
- 5 category fit and buyer expectation.

Any hard reject caps slot `01` score at 59 even if conversion appeal is high.

## Slots 02-09 Strategy

Supporting images can use text, scene, callouts, dimensions, and use cases if truthful and category-safe. Build a sequence that answers purchase doubts:

1. Confirm exact set/variant or alternate angle.
2. Explain size, scale, dimensions, capacity, or fit.
3. Prove material, texture, finish, durability cues, or construction.
4. Show core function or use process.
5. Show natural usage scenario.
6. Show installation, fold, storage, cleaning, or portability if relevant.
7. Compare included parts, package contents, or compatibility.
8. Use strongest remaining proof image only if not repetitive.

## Conversion Score

Use 0-100:

- 25 relevance to buyer doubt.
- 20 visible selling point strength.
- 15 clarity and professional appearance.
- 15 match to title/bullet evidence.
- 15 differentiation from other selected images.
- 10 A+ or gallery storytelling value.

Prefer fewer strong images over filling all 9 slots with weak or repetitive assets.
